Welcome to Noise & TerrainGen!

Noise & TerrainGen is a free online graph-based noise editor. Here's how to get started:

  • Use the left panel to create or select your graphs.
  • Add nodes to your graph and connect them to generate procedural noise and textures. Right-click or double-click to create new nodes.
  • Tweak node values to experiment with your creation in real time.
  • Enable Terrain mode to preview heightmaps.

Everything you create is automatically stored locally in your browser. You can also export your graphs anytime to save them permanently.

Have fun exploring and creating!
